HOW IT STARTED

when I started learning to develop games, I developed a game that was named "Wreck Race", Your goal was to get to the finish line before the time reaches zero and without getting wrecked. Unfortunately I suspended this project because the game had a lots of lag and I had no idea how to fix it.
Now i said "Why don't I make the game again? but this time it's better and more awesome"
I started making it.. And here it is!
